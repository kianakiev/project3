New York City, New York, USA

The city that never sleeps and my favorite place to experience new feelings.

Why I Love NYC

New York City is full of energy, history, culture, and amazing food. From famous skyscrapers to beautiful parks, it's a place where there’s always something new to discover. It's exciting, diverse, and powerful. When I have went it had always been an unforgettable experience, pulling me in and even creating a feeling of longing to move there. It has its own atmosphere that feels show stopping. It made me feel like I was on top of the world. I highly recommend paying a visit to this magical place.

Top Attractions

Statue of Liberty

An iconic symbol of freedom. You can visit the island, climb the many steps,take incredible photos and learn its fascinating history.

Central Park

A peaceful escape in the middle of a city that never sleeps. Walk, bike, row a boat, and take a carriage ride in this huge and beautiful park. A park known to be bigger than some towns in the US. Plus the central park zoo where you can experience just a piece of one of the most iconic childhood movies, Madagascar.

Times Square

Bright lights, giant screens, and tons of energy. A must-see, especially at night! Filled with some of the best stores and food around the world. Dont forget to get some amazing photos.

Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

See famous artworks from Van Gogh, Picasso, Warhol, and more. Experience a whole new world of art and immerse yourself in the mind of an art lover.

Fun Facts About NYC

  • Population: Over 8 million people live in one of America's largest cities.
  • Languages spoken: Over 180 laguages spoken all across New york city. Just walking five blocks youll hear all sorts of languages and accents from all over the world giving it a unique rythm and feeling of being all around the world at once. They even have diverse neighboorhoods but sometimes you can find some just filled with a community all speaking one language, all practicing one religion, and all living one way. its truly a place to experience it all.
  • Known for: Pizza, bagels, nighlife, and Wall Street. There is truly so much to visit and explore.
  • Weather: Winter in New York is cold and often snowy, with temperatures dropping below freezing and the city sparkling with holiday lights and ice skating rinks. The chill can be biting, but the festive vibe makes it magical. Summer in New York is hot, humid, and full of energy, with street festivals, rooftop events, and locals escaping to parks or beaches. It can feel like a sweaty oven, but the city truly comes alive.
  • Geography: Made up of 5 cities – Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island
